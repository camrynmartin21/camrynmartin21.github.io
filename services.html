DAITH

PLACEMENT: Daith piercings hug the inner cartilage of the ear

PRICING: $30 + plus the cost of the earring

HEALING TIME: 6-9 months

AFTERCARE: Keep the area clean and regularly disinfect with saline solution. Avoid harsh chemicals and don't sleep directly on the piercing until it's somewhat healed.

TRAGUS

PLACEMENT: The small piece of cartilage over the entrance to the ear canal

PRICING: $40 + the cost of the jewelry

HEALING TIME: 6-12 months

AFTERCARE: Clean twice a day with a sterile, saline-based solution. Avoid putting pressure on the area by not sleeping on the side with the earring until it heals.

STACKED LOBE

PLACEMENT: Most commonly found on the earlobe but can be placed anywhere on the ear.

PRICING: $25 per piercing + cost of jewelery

HEALING TIME: 6-8 weeks

AFTERCARE: Clean all piercings 2x daily with saline solution, avoid snagging piercings to avoid irritation.

HELIX

PLACEMENT: The outer cartilage of the ear.

PRICING: $40 + cost of jewelery

HEALING TIME: 3-6 months

AFTERCARE: Wash the piercing twice daily with a saline solution and avoid irritating it as much as possible.
